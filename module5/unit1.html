Unit 1

An Introduction to Security and Risk Management

Learning Outcomes

1. Discuss the various definitions of risk.
2. Explain how to assess, qualify and mitigate risks.
3. Describe various approaches to quantify and qualify risks.
4. List common security and risk standards and select the appropriate one(s) for a given situation.

Artefacts

This unit's formative activity was the collaborative discussion regarding risks of digitalization. My post can be found here.

Reflection

The first unit covered risk management and included a collaborative discussion on the risks of digitalization. I found the topic to be useful and relevant to the module, and the discussion was a good way to solidify my knowledge on the matter.
